About Us

The Book Nook is a dark academia themed tea shop and book shop located under the canopy of the famous Milk Market in the heart of Limerick City. It hosts a wide variety of teas from all over the world as well as various coffees, chocolate drinks, cold drinks, snacks, cakes and seasonal treats. The books in store are available to be purchased or checked out, to be read while you enjoy your drink. We have a small but varied collection of vintage books, able to be viewed and purchased by appointment only as some are in quite a delicate condition.
